Welcome to Zorin OS

Zorin OS is a specialized Linux distro with an Ubuntu-based base system which merges the power of Linux with a friendly user interface that is vastly Windows and macOS-like. It is designed to be simple to use, secure, and flexible for both novice and advanced users.

One of the most powerful strengths of Zorin OS is its ease of use. It is structured to cater to users of any and every level. The system's simple, intuitive nature makes new users feel incredibly at home immediately. At the same time, the complex options and enormous amount of customization possibilities can make it possible for anyone willing to go an extra step to tailor the system to their own workflow and needs.

Why Zorin OS?

For the average person, let alone those unfamiliar with computers, the transition to Linux can be very daunting with an unfamiliar system of installation of the product as well as with compatibility issues of their device. But Zorin OS, in fact, is specifically formulated to simplify all that. It bridges the divide by offering an environment that will be familiar as it leverages the power and security of Linux. Its effectiveness on old hardware makes it a good contender to revive older machines.

You can go into the various versions, ranging from the free Core version for general users to the commercial pro version for professional tools and full scale customizations, or even the Education version for students and teachers in any and all schools. Every version of Zorin OS is crafted to precise specifications so as to deliver optimal performance. Such quality focus on Zorin OS renders it a secure choice for an incredibly broad range of users. Additionally, Zorin OS has the ability to run Windows programs through applications such as wine, and this helps provide an even smoother experience for those who have not yet fully transitioned from Windows based applications. With continued development, and with complete documentation, Zorin OS provides a great computer experience that conforms to the incredibly broad range of user needs and requirements.
